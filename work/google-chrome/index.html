The keyboard cat came back!

A musical fun-fest to share the joy of JAM with Chrome

This animal-packed animation introduces JAM with Chrome, an interactive web app enabling friends to play music together in real time through their Chrome browser, wherever they are in the world. Featuring an animated spin on the legendary Keyboard Cat together with three animal pals getting their groove on, the video attracted five million YouTube views on its release.

Working with external illustrators and designers, we helped bring the characters to life by exploring and expanding their personalities with movement.
