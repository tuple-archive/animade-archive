Grab a template

Top tips

1. Hold the phone still and let it focus for best results

2. Make sure all 4 corner markers are in the frame. The screen will go green when it can see them all. Tap to play your animation.

3. Avoid corner markers from other templates being in the frame. The app will get confused and not be able to play your template.
